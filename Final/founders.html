About Our Founders

Disclaimer: The information on this page is entirely fictional and any resemblance to any form of reality is unlikely.

Jenni Singleton

Jenni was born in Scotland in 1853. She has always had a passion for developing technology and founded 1008Games on the advice of a psychic alien called Arnold who spoke to her in a dream.

Ami Chiroku

Ami got her start in a government lab, designing puzzles to test the intelligence of non-human life. Her talents have shaped 1008Games into the award-winning company it is today.

Sir Quentin Fitzhubert

Former maths teacher Quentin loves interpretive dance and owns the largest collection of monocles in Europe. He established 1008Games as a way to share his fondness for puzzle-based learning with the world.
