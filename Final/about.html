About Us

History

1008Games was founded in 2013 in a shed in Sheffield. We have since grown to employ 20 designers and other staff at our Somewhere offices.

Mission Statement

We aim to make fun, educational games available to all children. Our core values are:

  • Accessibility - our games are designed to be playable by as many people as possible.
  • Availability - all our content is free so that everyone can enjoy our games.
  • Teaching - we hire the brightest young talent to help people reach their full potential.
